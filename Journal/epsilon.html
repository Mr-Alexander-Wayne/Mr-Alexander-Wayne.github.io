Day Six

I think I think a lot. Yeah, you read that right

It may be my rebellious nature, but I always question the norm. I ponder on things that most people accept as facts. A mild example would be why the general assumption is that they are 30 days in a month. Technically, there are more '31 days' months than '30 days'. Venturing further, there is no real reason why there are 12 months in a year. I could as well have divided the revolution of the earth around the sun into two parts. And I wonder who created time. You might be confused and I understand that. Time (Minutes, Seconds, et cetera) is merely a result of the calculations based on the speed of light. I find it hilarious that we base our existence on this.

Nevertheless, I realize I may never find the answers to all of my questions and I've come to terms with that
