About Us

Who We Are

Pejaabs Catering Service started as a family-owned bakery in 2004. We were officially registered in 2007 and since then, we have been beating the expectations of our clients with our exquisite cuisines.

Our market knowledge and industry expertise spans over a decade and we've been offering cutting edge hospitality services to the satisfaction of our clients.

Our business covers five main areas:
  • CATERING
  • BAKERY
  • EVENT MANAGEMENT
  • RENTALS
  • TRAINING

We pride ourselves on upholding our core values:
  • SERVICE
  • EMPATHY
  • RESILIENCE
  • HONESTY
  • AFFORDABILITY
