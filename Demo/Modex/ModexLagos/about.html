Who We Are ●

What you should know.

Welcome to the Modex Guest House, a gracious, beautiful, listed, twin buildings in serene environment and ambience situated in the heart of Lagos.

Where are we?

We are ideally located at No. 34, Olakunle Selesi Street, Ajao estate, Lagos - Of Asafariogun, 3mins drive from Muritala International airport.

Capacity

Our Guest house has 14 rooms that comes in different sizes ranges from executive, supreme and classic. If every room were filled to capacity, we could host more than 250 guests. In a typical season, we average 10 guests per night. Our multipurpose conference room can accommodate more than 60 guests sited for social function and about a 100 sitting for corporate functions. The multipurpose hall also has four restrooms dedicated for its guests, a projector and a writing board. We also have a private lounge which is designed of international standard.

Layout

The guest house has rooms on both ground floor and the first floor, with the first floor consist of the multipurpose hall, the caf√©, 4 rooms as well as our lobby. It's a short stroll from the front desk.

Accessibility

Our guest house rooms vary in sizes. 2 ways steps if desired. An intercom telephone device in all the rooms that is directly linked to the front desk. All signs, including room numbers, on a standard number.

Transportation

Taxis are easily available for transportation within the city.
