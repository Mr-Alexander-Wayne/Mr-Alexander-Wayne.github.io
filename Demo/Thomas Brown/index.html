Mission Statement

Thomas Brown Comprehensive High School (TBCHS) is a citadel of learning that is registered and licensed under the educational board in Nigeria. TBCHS was established in 2009 as a secondary school that comprise of Junior Secondary School 1 - 3 and Senior Secondary School 1- 3. We are WAEC and NECO approved institution. Our school is managed by seasoned education specialist and offers both Nigeria and British curriculum.
