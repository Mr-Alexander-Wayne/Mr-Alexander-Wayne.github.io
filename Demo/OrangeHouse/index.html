Welcome to OrangeHouse Academy

We are an institution that prides ourselves in promoting academic as well as moral values

Located in the serene neighborhood of Rumuodara, we provide quality and affordable educational experience to our pupils. Our two storey building houses creche, preschool, nursery and primary classes. Our spacious playground coupled with our vast extracurricular activities ensure that we make learning fun and practical

Our staff to student ratio allows for more flexible, one-on-one interaction and focus on our pupils. Our field trips have given a new dimension to education and we have treated our students to visits to the Airport, Port Harcourt Mall as well as the Radio Station. We also engage our students in debates as well as arts and crafts
